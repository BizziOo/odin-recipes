Margherita

Description

Pizza Margherita (more commonly known in English as Margherita pizza) is a typical Neapolitan pizza, made with San Marzano tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, salt, and extra-virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

    For the dough

  • 500g ‘00’ or strong white flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 sachet (7g) fast-action dried yeast

    • For the sauce

  • 400g can plum tomatoes (San Marzano are the best)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to serve
  • 1⁄2 tsp dried oregano
  • small bunch basil, leaves picked, stalks reserved

    • For the toppings

  • 50g parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), freshly grated
  • 200g mozzarella from a block, cut into chunks
